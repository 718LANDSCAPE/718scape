Who We Are

718LANDSCAPE, LLC is a Georgia-based landscaping business rooted in passion, sustainability, and community. Our headquarters is located in Dunwoody, GA. Cryytal Gomez, the founder, led the company with a self-taught background, we’re proudly on the journey to becoming a certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE).

Our Mission

We don’t just cut grass — we raise the value of your property. Our mission is to elevate outdoor spaces through quality, eco-conscious landscaping while supporting job creation and mentorship in underserved communities.

What Makes Us Different

  • Founder-led & Vision-Driven: We lead with purpose, education, and experience.
  • Eco-Conscious: Native plants, smart water use, and sustainable designs.
  • Community Focus: Creating opportunities while improving curb appeal.
  • Long-Term Growth: Our services aim to build lasting impact and relationships.

Our Vision

718LANDSCAPE envisions becoming Georgia’s most trusted landscaping service for commercial, government, and private clients. With a commitment to excellence and community, we’re scaling with intention to become a preferred public vendor and premier outdoor expert.
