About Us

718LANDSCAPE, LLC is a Georgia-based landscaping company dedicated to high-quality, eco-conscious land care. Founded with the goal of elevating communities and landscapes simultaneously, our headquarters is proudly located in Dunwoody, GA. As a self-taught founder-led business, we are actively pursuing Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) certifications.

What We Do

  • Commercial Landscaping: Offices, retail complexes, corporate grounds
  • Private Estate and Airbnb Property Beautification
  • Government & Municipal Groundskeeping (cemeteries, parks, and public land)
  • Eco-Conscious Design & Native Plant Integration
  • Enhancements: Pressure Washing, Cleanup Services, Seasonal Upgrades

Our Experts & Strategic Partners

Although a startup, 718LANDSCAPE is guided by a professional network of industry veterans. Our partners include certified landscape architects, horticulturists, irrigation system specialists, arborists, and environmental consultants. These collaborations provide mentorship, compliance assurance, and the technical backbone for scalable, expert-driven service delivery. Together, we maintain a firm commitment to evolving our craft through continual learning and innovation.
